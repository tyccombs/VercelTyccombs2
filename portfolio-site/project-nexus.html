Case Study

Nexus:
A Commercial Fashion Film

Director

Elena Rossi

Cinematography

Tobias Haardt

Editor

Soo-Jin Park

Studio

Lumina Films

The Synopsis

Nexus is a high-concept fashion film for a luxury tech brand, exploring the intersection of machine precision and human desire. Shot in a decommissioned server farm, the film blurs the line between interface and body.

Director's Note

I wanted the garments to feel like they were wearing the space — not the other way around. The architecture was the costume.

  • movieCaptured on location with minimal crew.
  • moviePractical effects and in-camera techniques throughout.
  • movieOriginal score composed specifically for picture.

The Aesthetic

Ultra-clean whites interrupted by deep blacks. Symmetrical compositions with surgical camera movements. Every frame designed to look like a UI element.
