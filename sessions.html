Featured Project

Live Performance Session

A raw and intimate look at a live studio session, capturing the energy and emotion of the performance in a single take.

Vision & Concept

Director's Note

My vision for this session was to strip away the artifice of modern production and focus purely on the visceral connection between the artist and the music. We wanted the audience to feel as if they were standing in the room, inches away from the instruments.

Using a single-take approach, we avoided the safety of multiple angles to force a performance that felt immediate and high-stakes. The lighting was designed to be minimal, letting the shadows frame the intensity of the moment.
